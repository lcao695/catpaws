    Kewpie Baby Series

    I created this series initially out of boredom but continued when I unexpectedly found joy and positivity in the junction of plastic baby dolls and landscape photos I had taken, layered with sometimes motivational and sometimes cryptic messages over top.

    Desktop Anthology, May 2024

    This desktop anthology is a collection of the miscellaneous unsorted objects existing on my dorm room desk by the end of my first year of college. I created this collage to memorialize my first year and use it as a time capsule to look back upon when freshman year is behind me.
